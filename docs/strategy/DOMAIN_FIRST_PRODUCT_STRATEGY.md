# Vendas Ativas — Domain-First Product Strategy

## Philosophy

**DOMAIN-FIRST.**

We preserve Karine/GrowBiz operational workflow patterns during prototype validation.

### Current Priority
- workflow truth
- operational realism
- usability
- identifying automation opportunities

### NOT Current Priority
- generic SaaS abstraction
- over-normalized architecture
- multi-tenant generalization
- configurable workflow engines

---

## Prototype Phase Rules

### Preserve
- Karine's operational terminology
- Real service flows (Imigrou/GrowBiz products)
- Real checklist logic (gravação, social, evento)
- Operational sales stages (12 statuses)
- Creator/media business patterns

### Avoid
- Generic CRM language ("opportunities", "deals")
- Abstract enterprise terminology
- Premature template engines
- Premature configurability

---

## Goal

Validate whether this operational model creates real value before abstracting into a SaaS platform.

### Validation Criteria
1. Can Karine use this daily without confusion?
2. Does it reflect her actual workflow?
3. Does it reduce cognitive load?
4. Does it surface actionable insights?
5. Does it identify real automation opportunities?

---

## Future SaaS Strategy (Phase 2+)

1. **Validate GrowBiz workflow** ← WE ARE HERE
2. Identify repeatable operational patterns
3. Abstract into templates/configuration

Only after validation:
- Multi-tenancy
- Configurable workflows
